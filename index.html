Check the strength of your Password

This app cross checks your password with the already leaked and publically available passwords set, without compormising yours, using k anonimity model.

Why is it important?

We have a tendency to reuse passwords or use common and easy to remember passwords. So it is a good idea to verify that our password is not already exposed during any data breach. If we are using already exposed passwords, then you are facing a huge risk of compromising your account to a simple dictionary attack.We have a tendency to reuse passwords or use common and easy to remember passwords.
